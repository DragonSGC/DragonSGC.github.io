About Me


My name is Simon Crampton and I am a passionate self-taught developer. I have always been intrested in technology from a young age, dabbled with code in my studies and fully started my journey two years ago. I have an excellent foundation in Python as well as a certification from AWS on cloud fundamentals while I continue my journey with various cloud certifications.

The world of Python and software development is an ever increasing one with new frameworks, packages, methodologies and technologies that push the industry forward. The challenge to continually learn and grow oneself to meet this change is something I am and have commited to taking upon myself. To learn is to grow, the industry demands self improvement, to improve and drive oneself forward with the industy.

I am happiest while behind my code editor, trying to derive a solution to a problem, or winning the battle against an obscure bug that has casued you endless issues. I am always eager to learn and grow, and I believe that the best way to do this is to learn from those around me and always have a thirst to obtain knowledge. I will always encourage engagement amongst my peers and see evvery engagement as a learning opportunity.

Besides that aspect of my professional life, I have been an avid gamer for the last 25 years and I love the world of gaming, to be asbsorbed within the creative world of a game to discover all that the game has to offer and experience it is something that I dearly love. I am also a keen reader, I love being lost in a fantasy novel or gripped in the world of a sci-fi thriller. I also love travelling going to places that are intresting and not necessarily popular attractions, discovering the history of the place and experiencing the culture that my destination has to offer.
