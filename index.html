About Me


My name is Simon Crampton, and I am a passionate self-taught developer. I have had a keen interest in technology from a young age, during my studies my passion was ignited for software development as I delved into coding. In 2022 I restarted my journey in earnest. I have an excellent foundation in Python backed by a certification from AWS on cloud fundamentals while I continue my journey with various other cloud certifications.

The world of Python and software development is an ever-changing landscape with new frameworks, packages, methodologies and technologies that push the industry forward. As a software developer I enjoy the challenge of keeping pace with the changing nature of the industry. To learn is to grow, the industry demands self-improvement, to improve and drive oneself forward with the industry.

I am at my happiest while immersed in a codebase, deriving a solution to a problem, or winning the battle against an obscure bug that has caused endless issues. I am also eager to learn from others and have the view that no matter the experience every engagement with peers is an opportunity to gain knowledge. I look forward to seizing those opportunities whenever they happen.

Besides that aspect of my professional life, I have been an avid gamer for the last 25 years, to be absorbed within the creative world of a game to discover all that the game has to offer and experience, it is something that I dearly love. I am also a keen reader, I love being lost in a fantasy novel or gripped in the world of a sci-fi thriller. I actually do escape the four walls of my home and love travelling to places that have a diverse and interesting history or culture that may not necessarily be on the main road so to speak. My passion for travel is matched by that for experiencing new foods, often with those two coinciding.
